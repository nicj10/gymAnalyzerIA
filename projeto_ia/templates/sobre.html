About Us

Gym Analyzer is a revolutionary platform that uses artificial intelligence and data analysis to transform gym management. Based on recent studies by IHRSA (International Health, Racquet & Sportsclub Association), gyms that implement data analysis see an average 30% increase in member retention and a 25% boost in operational efficiency.

Our solution provides valuable insights into attendance patterns, peak hours, and member behavior, enabling managers to make data-driven decisions to optimize resources and enhance the member experience.

Data Used

We use a comprehensive set of gym data, including attendance information, class bookings, and equipment usage patterns. Our analysis is based on real data from Canadian gyms, processed and anonymized to ensure user privacy.
